## ✨ Features

### 📸 AI Food Detective (Gemini Vision)
Snap a photo of your meal and let **Google Gemini** instantly analyze:
- ✅ **Calories** and **Protein** estimates
- ✅ **Dish Name** recognition
- ✅ **Fun facts** and a witty food roast
- **PWA Optimized Camera**: Works seamlessly on mobile and desktop.
- **GPS Integration**: Automatically detects where you ate (optional). If location metadata is missing from the photo, the app will request your current GPS position.

### 🐼 The Panda Coach
Interact with a sassy, motivational, and adorable Panda coach.
- **AI-Powered Advice**: Powered by Gemini — gives a unique, personalized comment every time based on your actual calorie, protein, and water data.
- **🔥 Streak Tracker**: Tracks how many consecutive days you've logged your diet.

### 📸 Daily War Report (Sharing Card)
Generate a beautiful, shareable summary card of your day.
- One tap to create a **Neo-brutalist style image** perfect for Threads or Instagram Stories.
- Includes: date, streak, Panda Coach quote, and progress bars for calories, protein and water.

### 💧 Smart Tracking
- **Water Log**: Quick 1-tap tracking to keep you hydrated.
- **Daily Summary**: Visualize your progress with intuitive circular metrics.
- **Weight Tracker**: Log and monitor your weight over time.
- **History Log**: A beautiful accordion list of all past records.

### 🎨 Premium Design & UX
- **Neo-brutalist Aesthetic**: Bold borders, vibrant yellow accents, and clean typography.
- **Smooth Animations**: Powered by Framer Motion.
- **Multilingual**: Supports **Traditional Chinese (繁體中文)** and **English**.
- **Auto-Update**: Detects new versions on every visit and refreshes automatically.

## 🚀 Get Started

Visit the app directly on your phone or computer:  
👉 **[https://winnie-lin.space/daily-diet/](https://winnie-lin.space/daily-diet/)**

### 📱 Install as App (PWA)
1. Open the link in **Safari** (iOS) or **Chrome** (Android).
2. Tap **Share** or **Menu**.
3. Select **"Add to Home Screen"**.
4. Enjoy a native app experience!

## 🛠 Tech Stack

| Layer | Technology |
|-------|-----------|
| Framework | [React](https://reactjs.org/) + [Vite](https://vitejs.dev/) |
| Styling | [Tailwind CSS](https://tailwindcss.com/) |
| Animations | [Framer Motion](https://www.framer.com/motion/) |
| Database | [Dexie.js](https://dexie.org/) (IndexedDB) |
| AI Engine | [Google Gemini](https://deepmind.google/technologies/gemini/) (Vision + Text) |
| Image Export | [html2canvas](https://html2canvas.hertzen.com/) |
| Deployment | GitHub Pages (via GitHub Actions) |
