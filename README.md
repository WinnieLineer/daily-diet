## ✨ Features

### 📸 AI Food Detective (Vision)
Snap a photo of your meal and let our AI instantly analyze the **Calories**, **Protein**, and **Dish Name**. 
- **PWA Optimized Camera**: Works seamlessly on mobile and desktop.
- **GPS Integration**: Automatically detects where you ate (optional).

### 🐼 The Panda Coach
Interact with a sassy, motivational, and adorable Panda coach.
- **Dynamic Dialogues**: Gets to know your eating habits.
- **Feedback System**: Send messages to the developer directly through the app.

### 💧 Smart Tracking
- **Water Log**: Quick 1-tap tracking to keep you hydrated.
- **Daily Summary**: Visualize your progress with intuitive circular metrics.
- **History Log**: A beautiful list of everything you've recorded today.

### 🎨 Premium Design & UX
- **Neo-brutalist Aesthetic**: Bold borders, vibrant colors (Glassmorphism).
- **Smooth Animations**: Powered by Framer Motion.
- **Multilingual**: Supports **Traditional Chinese (繁體中文)** and **English**.
- **Hard Cache Busting**: Always ensures you're using the latest version.

## 🚀 Get Started

Visit the app directly on your phone or computer:  
👉 **[https://winnie-lin.space/daily-diet/](https://winnie-lin.space/daily-diet/)**

### 📱 Install as App (PWA)
1. Open the link in **Safari** (iOS) or **Chrome** (Android).
2. Tap **Share** or **Menu**.
3. Select **"Add to Home Screen"**.
4. Enjoy a native app experience!

## 🛠 Tech Stack

- **Framework**: [React](https://reactjs.org/) + [Vite](https://vitejs.dev/)
- **Styling**: [Tailwind CSS](https://tailwindcss.com/)
- **Animations**: [Framer Motion](https://www.framer.com/motion/)
- **AI Engine**: OpenAI GPT-4 Vision (via custom API)
- **Deployment**: GitHub Pages / Static Hosting
